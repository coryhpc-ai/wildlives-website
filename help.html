Volunteer With Us

Wildlives is dependent upon volunteer help. We need more volunteers, especially now the summer season is coming and we expect many orphans.

The work involves cleaning, feeding and caring for a wide range of animals. No previous experience is necessary — full training is given.

Who We Need

Hard-working, dependable people who love animals and enjoy working outdoors.

What You’ll Do

Cleaning, feeding, and caring for wildlife. Summer work is varied and unpredictable.

The Benefits

Gain valuable hands-on experience with wildlife. Perfect for students or anyone passionate about nature.

How to Apply

Ring Rosie on 01206 251174 to arrange a visit.

More About Us

Our Story
Wildlives was set up by Rosie Catford in September 1995. From a few boxes in her conservatory, we have grown into a six-acre rescue centre with purpose-built pens, an animal hospital, and a nature reserve.
