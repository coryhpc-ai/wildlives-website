If you find an injured or sick animal, act immediately.

Before handling any wild animal, there are important principles you must be aware of. Please take a moment to read the guidance below before intervening.

What To Do

Stay Calm

Keep your distance and avoid stress to the animal.

Do Not Treat

Do not attempt to feed or treat injuries yourself.

Keep Safe

If possible, place the animal somewhere quiet and contained.

Call For Help

Contact us immediately for professional advice.
